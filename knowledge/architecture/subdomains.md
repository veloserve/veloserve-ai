# Proposed Product Surfaces

- `veloserve.io` - main product site and downloads
- `platform.veloserve.io` - customer SaaS portal
- `community.veloserve.io` - community experience
- `licensing.veloserve.io` - licensing platform and validation APIs

## Notes

- `velopanel` is a product brand and may be distributed as software rather than only as a single central SaaS hostname.
- `platform` should avoid owning low-level hosting panel operations.
- `licensing` should remain callable by both `veloserve` and `velopanel`.
